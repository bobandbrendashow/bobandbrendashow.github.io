Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13th December 2025

TikTok Uploader respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • TikTok OAuth credentials (access and refresh tokens) required to upload and publish content on your behalf.
  • Basic account identifiers returned by TikTok (such as an account ID) to associate credentials with the correct user.
  • Video files that you upload for the purpose of publishing to TikTok.

How we use your information

  • To authenticate you with TikTok.
  • To upload and publish the video you explicitly request.
  • To maintain an authenticated session so you do not need to re-authorise repeatedly.

Storage and security

OAuth tokens are stored securely and used only to perform actions you initiate. Uploaded videos are stored temporarily for processing and are deleted after upload completes or within a short retention window.

Sharing of information

We do not sell or rent your data. Information is shared only with TikTok via TikTok’s official APIs to complete the requested upload or publish action.

Your choices

  • You may revoke access at any time through TikTok’s account settings.
  • You may request deletion of stored credentials by contacting us.
